Privacy Policy

OneTime does not collect, share, or sell any user infomation. OneTime does not have any remote servers, nor does the app ever connect to the Internet. All information is stored on-device or in Apple Keychain (Apple Privacy Policy).
Users that have opted into Share Analytics with App Developers in the Privacy tab in Settings should refer to the Apple Privacy Policy above. Analytics provided to OneTime developers from this mechanism are never shared with outside parties.
